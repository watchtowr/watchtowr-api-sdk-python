# FindingImpactThreshold

Impact Setting:
* High - Prioritized Findings (vulnerabilities, misconfigurations and weaknesses) that could have an immediate, direct impact on your organization's security posture.
* All - All Findings, a broader range of findings that may not directly impact your organization's security posture, but may represent deviations from best practices.


## Values

| Name   | Value  |
| ------ | ------ |
| `ALL`  | All    |
| `HIGH` | High   |
